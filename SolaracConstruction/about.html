About SOLARAC Construction LTD

Building the future with integrity, innovation, and excellence

Company Profile

SOLARAC Company Limited (RC:1175907) is a Limited Liability Company incorporated to deliver expertise in Project Management, Civil Engineering and Construction, General Facilities Management, and General Contract Services. Based in Osogbo, Osun State, we have built a reputation for delivering high-quality projects that meet international standards.

We are managed by a team of professionals with diverse backgrounds and expertise in Project Management, Designing, Contracting, Civil Engineering and Construction Services, Engineering Services, Facility Maintenance, and Project finance management.

At SOLARAC, we're passionate about Civil engineering and committed to precision in Project Delivery, ensuring high-quality results for our clients. As a registered construction company, we are committed to maintaining the highest standards of professionalism, safety, and environmental responsibility in all our operations.
